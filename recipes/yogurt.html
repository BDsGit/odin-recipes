Ben's Recipe for Homemade Yogurt

This is a quick recipe I just tried out for making homemade yogurt if you have an Instant Pot!

First, let's start with the list of ingredients that you'll need to make this recipe

  • Some milk (preferably whole, though lower fat percentages will work too
  • A small amount of your favourite yogurt (this will be used as the starter)

Since the recipe is so simple, it's really just a few steps:

  1. Thoroughly clean the inside of your Instant Pot
  2. Add all your milk to the pot, select the yogurt function, and set it to boil
  3. Allow the milk to cool
  4. Add a small amount of your starter yogurt, and mix it in well
  5. Select the yogurt function again, but this time set it to low for 8 hours.
And that's all, after the 8 hours is up, you're done! Enjoy your yogurt plain,with added fruit, sugar, or anything that you like!
